# NEO-CORP Military E-commerce - Constitution

## Mission
To provide a secure, high-performance digital terminal for the requisition and management of advanced military-grade tactical equipment and neural-link hardware.

## Core Principles
1. **Security First:** All requisition actions and data transfers must be authenticated and encrypted via high-grade protocols.
2. **Immersive Aesthetic:** Maintain a professional "techy" military look that reflects the high-tech nature of the hardware.
3. **AI-Driven Intelligence:** Empower operators with real-time technical intelligence and support using state-of-the-art AI.
